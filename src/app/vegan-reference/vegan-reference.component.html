Egg Alternatives

You'll also be surprised at how much you just don't need eggs for. Some recipes are fine if you just remove the egg altogether — such is the case with Latkes.

You can also get creative with other substitutes like vinegar, vegan buttermilk, and other pureed fruits like avocado. One time I even made a cake with pepsi instead of egg and milk. It wasn't the most flavorful cake but it worked!

Below are the most common substitutes we use.

Replacement 1 Egg Equivalent Use
Flax 1 T flaxmeal + 2.5 T water Cookies, breads, pancakes, waffles, most things
Ener-G Egg Replacer .5 t powder + 2 T water Cookies, breads, pancakes, waffles, most things
Whole chia seeds 1 T seeds + 3 T water Waffles, muffins
Banana ½ medium banana, mashed Brownies, muffins, waffles, pancakes
Apple sauce ¼ cup Brownies, muffins, waffles, pancakes, cookies
Silken tofu ¼ cup, pureed Cakes, cupcakes, muffins, bars, brownies
Chickpea juice/Aquafaba 3 T, whipped or not Meringue, cookies, breads, pancakes, waffles, most things, coating breads, challah
Nut milk ¼ cup Cakes, cupcakes, muffins, coating breads, challah
